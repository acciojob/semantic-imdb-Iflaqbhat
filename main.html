Summary

"Inception" is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie explores the concept of "dream hacking," where skilled individuals can enter and manipulate the dreams of others. Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, is a thief with the ability to infiltrate the subconscious of his targets. Tasked with planting an idea in a target's mind, Cobb and his team navigate complex dreamscapes that blur the line between reality and fantasy.
