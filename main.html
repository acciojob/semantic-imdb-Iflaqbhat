Summary

"Swades" is a Bollywood drama film directed by Ashutosh Gowariker. It tells the story of Mohan Bhargava, a successful NASA scientist played by Shah Rukh Khan, who returns to India to find his childhood nanny. During his journey, he experiences the realities of rural India, including its poverty, caste discrimination, and the struggles of its people. Inspired by their resilience, Mohan decides to use his skills to make a difference, ultimately leading a project to bring electricity to the village through a hydroelectric power plant. The film is a poignant tale of self-discovery and the importance of giving back to one's roots.
