Summary

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan. It is the second film in Nolan's Batman trilogy and follows the story of Batman as he tries to stop the Joker, a criminal mastermind who wants to plunge Gotham City into anarchy. The film explores complex themes such as justice, morality, and chaos, with intense performances by Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger as the Joker.

Actors

  • Christian Bale as Bruce Wayne / Batman
  • Heath Ledger as The Joker
  • Aaron Eckhart as Harvey Dent / Two-Face
  • Michael Caine as Alfred Pennyworth
  • Maggie Gyllenhaal as Rachel Dawes
