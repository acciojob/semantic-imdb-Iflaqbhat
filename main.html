Plot Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a thief who specializes in the art of "extraction", stealing secrets from within a person's subconscious. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform the impossible task of "inception" â€” planting an idea in someone's mind. However, as the team dives deeper into the layers of the dream world, Cobb must confront his own inner demons.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio stars as Dom Cobb, the troubled thief who leads the team of dream extractors.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt plays Arthur, Cobb's trusted partner and fellow extractor.

Ellen Page portrays Ariadne, the brilliant architect who helps build the dream worlds.

Tom Hardy plays Eames, a forger who specializes in impersonation within dreams.
